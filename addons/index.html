Add-ons

Add-ons are a way of the community introducing custom mobs to PetsMod, additionally allowing us to provide official add-ons without having to over-bloat the initial mod.

Offical Add-ons

Official add-ons are provided and endorsed by the Pets Team.

Unofficial Addons

Unofficial addons are made by members of the community or members of the Pets Team, but not officially endorsed by the Pets Team. However, We believe highlighting user creations is important - please let us know on our GitHub if you want your creation on this list!
